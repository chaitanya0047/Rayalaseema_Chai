About

Welcome to Rayalaseema Chai!

Rayalaseema Chai is known for its rich heritage and unique blend of local spices with strong black tea. Its distinctive flavor comes from traditional preparation methods and indigenous ingredients, making it a true symbol of Rayalaseema’s culinary pride.

"Rayalaseema Chai isn't just tea—it's an experience that brings the warmth and spice of home to every cup."

Overview and Heritage: Rayalaseema Chai is more than just a tea house; it’s a celebration of the rich cultural heritage of Rayalaseema, a historic region in Andhra Pradesh, India. Established in 1995, we have been serving authentic, handcrafted chai that captures the bold flavors and rustic charm of the region. Our name, "Rayalaseema," reflects the land of kings, and our chai embodies the warmth and hospitality of its people.

Unique Flavors: At Rayalaseema Chai, every cup tells a story. Our signature blends, like the fiery Masala Chai infused with freshly ground spices, the soothing Ginger Chai that warms the soul, and the refreshing Lemon Tea, are crafted using traditional recipes passed down through generations. We source the finest tea leaves and natural ingredients directly from local farmers, ensuring every sip bursts with authenticity and freshness.

Ambiance and Experience: Step into Rayalaseema Chai, and you’re welcomed by the aroma of brewing tea and the nostalgic vibes of a traditional South Indian tea stall. Our space is designed to evoke memories of simpler times—think clay cups, rustic seating, and the rhythmic sound of chai being poured. Whether you’re catching up with friends or seeking a quiet moment, our tea house offers a haven of comfort and connection.

Our Mission: We aim to preserve the art of chai-making while bringing people together over a shared love for this timeless beverage. Rayalaseema Chai isn’t just about drinking tea; it’s about experiencing a piece of history, a dash of tradition, and a whole lot of heart in every cup. We strive to be a place where stories are shared, laughter echoes, and memories are brewed.

Community and Sustainability: Rooted in the values of Rayalaseema, we are committed to supporting our local community. From employing local artisans to sourcing sustainable ingredients, we believe in giving back to the land that inspires us. Our eco-friendly practices ensure that your chai experience is not only delightful but also kind to the environment.

Why Choose Us: What sets Rayalaseema Chai apart is our unwavering dedication to quality and tradition. Each blend is prepared with care, ensuring a perfect balance of flavors that linger long after the last sip. Whether you’re a chai connoisseur or a first-timer, our diverse menu offers something for everyone, paired with warm smiles and exceptional service
